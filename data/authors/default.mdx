**Dániel Terbe** is an AI consultant and developer based in Budapest, Hungary, with a deep passion for blending the worlds of physics, neuroscience, and artificial intelligence. After earning a **BSc in Physics from Eötvös Loránd University**, he shifted his focus from understanding the universe to exploring the intricacies of the human mind, completing an **MSc in Info-Bionics from Pázmány Péter Catholic University**.

Over the years, Dániel has worked on a variety of innovative projects, ranging from remote pulse estimation using everyday cameras to developing machine learning systems for digital holographic microscopy. His professional career at the **Institute for Computer Science and Control (HUN-REN SZTAKI)** has allowed him to dive deep into AI, particularly in computer vision and deep learning, contributing to real-world applications like health monitoring and water sample analysis.

When he’s not developing cutting-edge AI solutions, Dániel enjoys winter sports, mountain biking, meditating, and jamming with friends, balancing his technical expertise with creative and physical pursuits.
